# PostPal Privacy Policy
- Purpose: Generate LinkedIn comments from page content the user selects.
- Data collected: Only the page text you select and settings you configure (API base URL, model, tone). No personal identifiers; not sold/shared.
- Use: Sends selected text to your backend at https://linkdin-comment-generater.onrender.com to generate comments; settings stored in chrome.storage.
- No analytics, no ads, no third-party tracking.
